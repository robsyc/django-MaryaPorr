About MaryaPorr

Maryam and her promotors at Ghent University

Our Mission

At MaryaPorr, we're on a bold mission: to end childhood malnutrition once and for all. Based in Belgium but with our hearts in Kenya and eyes on the world, we're pioneering innovative, insect-fortified instant foods that are nutritious, sustainable, and culturally relevant.

Our approach

We believe in turning research into real-world impact. That's why MaryaPorr isn't just about innovative food products; it's about bridging the gap between academic findings and practical solutions. Our insect-fortified instant foods are:

  • Highly Nutritious: Packed with quality proteins, fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals
  • Child-safe: Suitable for children of 6 month and older
  • Sustainable: Insect farming has a significantly lower environmental impact
  • Culturally Appropriate: Building on existing dietary practices in many parts of the world
  • Accessible: Designed to be affordable and easy to prepare

Our Founder

Maryam Imbumi is more than just our founder; she's the heart and soul of MaryaPorr. As a PhD student, nutritionist, and socio-economist born and raised in Western Kenya, she brings a unique blend of academic rigor and real-world understanding to our mission. Her personal connection to the issue of malnutrition drives our passionate approach to solving this global challenge.

Our Commitment

At MaryaPorr, we're committed to ensuring our work goes beyond the laboratory. We're dedicated to:

  • Continuous research and development to improve our products
  • Social impact and sustainability through biodegradable packaging
  • Collaborating with local communities to ensure our solutions are culturally appropriate and truly beneficial
  • Partnering with NGOs, governments, and other stakeholders to reach the children who need our help the most
  • Maintaining transparency in our processes and impact

Join us in our journey to nourish the future, one child at a time. Together, we can make malnutrition a thing of the past.
